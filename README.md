# Visitor Portfolio Risk Diagnostic

An interactive analytical diagnostic built using publicly available Tourism Research Australia National Visitor Survey (NVS) data for the Year Ending December 2024.

Rather than reporting visitor expenditure alone, the tool compares each Australian state's visitor expenditure portfolio against structurally similar destination peer groups to identify where individual visitor purposes are underrepresented, broadly aligned or more concentrated than comparable destinations.

The diagnostic is intended to support discussion around visitor portfolio structure, diversification and the next questions for strategic investigation.

---

## Visitor purposes

- Holiday
- Visiting Friends and Relatives (VFR)
- Business
- Other

## Method

The diagnostic compares visitor expenditure shares within destination-type peer groups using expenditure-weighted benchmarks.

Differences of ±0.5 percentage points or less are treated as broadly at benchmark.

The analysis is based on a single year of publicly available data and is intended as a directional diagnostic to support discussion and further investigation. It should not be interpreted as an official Tourism Research Australia publication, definitive forecast or policy recommendation.
