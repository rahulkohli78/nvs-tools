# Visitor Portfolio Risk Diagnostic

An interactive diagnostic built using publicly available Tourism Research Australia National Visitor Survey (NVS) data for the Year Ending December 2024.

Rather than reporting visitor expenditure alone, the tool compares each Australian state's visitor portfolio against structurally similar destination peer groups to identify where individual visitor purposes are underrepresented, broadly aligned or more concentrated than comparable destinations.

The diagnostic supports discussion around visitor portfolio structure, diversification and the next questions for strategic investigation.

## Visitor purposes

- Holiday
- Visiting Friends and Relatives (VFR)
- Business
- Other

## Data source

Tourism Research Australia (TRA)  
National Visitor Survey (NVS)  
Year Ending December 2024

The underlying NVS dataset is publicly available and remains the property of Tourism Research Australia. This repository does not claim ownership of the source data.

## Method

The diagnostic compares visitor expenditure shares within destination-type peer groups using expenditure-weighted benchmarks.

Differences of ±0.5 percentage points or less are treated as broadly at benchmark.

The analysis is based on a single year of data and should be interpreted as a directional diagnostic rather than a definitive forecast or policy recommendation.
