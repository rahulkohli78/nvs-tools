# Visitor Portfolio Risk Diagnostic

An interactive analytical diagnostic built using publicly available Tourism Research Australia National Visitor Survey (NVS) data for the Year Ending December 2024.

**Live tool:** https://rahulkohli78.github.io/nvs-tools/

## What it does

Rather than reporting visitor expenditure alone, the tool compares each Australian state's visitor expenditure portfolio with structurally similar destination peer groups.

It identifies where individual visitor purposes are underrepresented, broadly aligned or more concentrated than they are in comparable destinations.

The diagnostic is intended to support discussion around visitor portfolio structure, diversification and the next questions for strategic investigation.

## Visitor purposes

- Holiday
- Visiting Friends and Relatives (VFR)
- Business
- Other

## Data source

Tourism Research Australia (TRA)  
National Visitor Survey (NVS)  
Year Ending December 2024

The underlying National Visitor Survey dataset is publicly available and remains the property of Tourism Research Australia. This repository does not claim ownership of the source data.

## Method

The diagnostic compares visitor expenditure shares within destination-type peer groups using expenditure-weighted benchmarks.

The destination types used in the analysis are:

- **Urban Gateway:** New South Wales, Victoria and the Australian Capital Territory
- **Coastal Leisure:** Queensland and Tasmania
- **Regional Mixed:** South Australia and Western Australia
- **Remote:** Northern Territory

The Northern Territory is the sole Remote destination in the dataset. It is therefore not assessed against a peer-group concentration benchmark.

Absolute differences of 0.5 percentage points or less are treated as broadly at benchmark.

The analysis is based on a single year of publicly available data and is intended as a directional diagnostic to support discussion and further investigation.

It should not be interpreted as an official Tourism Research Australia publication, a definitive forecast or a policy recommendation.
